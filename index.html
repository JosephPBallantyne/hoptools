Document Controller

Automated Drawing Reviewer for Autodesk Revit — run your full QA checklist across every sheet with one click. Supports Revit 2024, 2025, and 2026.

Document Controller runs your full QA checklist across every sheet in your Revit project. Title block fields, revision dates, sheet numbers, viewport bounds, overlapping text, duplicate tags, grid alignment and more — checked in seconds. One install, three Revit versions, configurable to your office standards.

View on Autodesk App Store Get in touch
Sheet number validation

Checks sheet number format against a custom regex pattern.

Title block completeness

Project Number, Project Name, Drawn By, Checked By, Issue Date, Revision Date, and any parameter you specify.

Revision validation

Checks revision number, date, description, and chronological order.

Spell check

Covers text annotations, dimension overrides, and title block parameters. Architecture-aware dictionaries reduce false positives.

Colour compliance

Enforce Black & White, Black/White/Red, or unrestricted policies for tender, planning, or consultant submissions.

Viewport bounds

Flags titles or boxes extending outside the sheet boundary.

Text overlap detection

Finds annotations that overlap each other or overlap viewport boxes.

Duplicate annotation tags

Flags elements tagged more than once, including rooms and spaces.

Visible reference planes

Flags views where reference planes are still showing.

Grid alignment

Checks that grid bubbles are consistently aligned across viewports on the same sheet.

Information extraction

Pulls sheet metadata across selected sheets into a single summary, exportable to PDF.

Configurable rules

All check rules live in plain JSON files in AppData. Edit, share, or version-control them to enforce firm-wide standards.
