Pocket Copy

A minimal, privacy-first clipboard manager for macOS that lives in your menu bar. All data stored locally on your Mac.

Pocket Copy sits quietly in your menu bar, keeping a history of everything you copy without ever sending a byte off your machine. No subscriptions, no accounts, no cloud — just your clipboard, always within reach.
